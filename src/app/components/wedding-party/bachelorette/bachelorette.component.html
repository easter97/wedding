Outfit Inspiration
tuesday

Think beachy elegance with a romantic twist. Dress in neutrals, soft metallics like gold or mother-of-pearl, and embrace textures like raffia, seashell accents, or crochet.

Flowy fabrics, beachy accessories, and sunset-ready glam are perfect for this dreamy seaside vibe.

wednesday

It’s boat day! Time to break out your blue swimsuits—any shade goes, from sky to navy. Patterns and metallics are totally welcome too.

For this evening, no specific theme. Just wear something cute and fun that you feel good in!

thursday

For the grand finale, we’re going all black to mourn the death of Amanda's single life. Go bold, go glam, go a little edgy—just make sure it’s head-to-toe black.

These themes are totally optional! Please don’t feel pressure to buy anything new, just do your best with what you already have. The goal is to have fun and feel good. Only one request: please avoid wearing white—save that for the bride!
