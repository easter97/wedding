The Wedding Weekend

    Please join us on Saturday, October 11th at 4:00 PM on the south beach as we exchange vows.

  • Cocktail Hour

    Immediately following the ceremony, enjoy cocktails and light bites by the beach.

  • Reception

    At 6:00 PM, we’ll gather on the Oceana Terrace for dinner, toasts, and dancing under the stars.
